ACFA LEAGUE

Register your club

The ACFA League connects chess communities through organized club competition while allowing each club to preserve its own identity and home community.

Team format

League events are club-versus-club competitions. Event rosters generally use a minimum of three and maximum of ten participating players, with backup players permitted between events.

Featured competition

Club Daily Matches are the main league format, supported by Vote Chess, arenas, Swiss tournaments, and approved special events.

Club representation

Players represent their organic club. A player already participating in an active daily match cannot be replaced during that match.
